About:
Gabriela d’Amato is a designer and sound producer from Brooklyn, NY. She works across several media – from magazines & identities to installations & recordings. Her creative style operates best within multidisciplinary collaborations, where clarity outshines cleverness. She is passionate about her experiences – finding guidance in patterns & repetition, expressive cultures, and experimental music.
